Steps From the Golden Mount

The Chedee sits at the foot of Wat Saket's iconic golden stupa, in a quiet corner of Old Bangkok where temple bells, riverside markets, and hand-painted shophouses remain unchanged by time. Climb the mount at dawn for the best view of the city, then return to modern comfort within minutes.
